## Manifest and discovery

- Use `.well-known/farcaster.json` metadata.
- App search/indexing depends on manifest quality and recrawl behavior.
- Re-sharing URL helps with fresh indexing after updates.

Source:
- https://docs.base.org/mini-apps/core-concepts/manifest
- https://docs.base.org/mini-apps/troubleshooting/how-search-works
